# ADR-005: AAARTS strategy dashboard ontology

Status: accepted

## Decision

The public dashboard is strategy-centered. A versioned `StrategyVersion` is the
parent object; `Hypothesis`, `ImplementationVariant`, `ResearchRun`,
`EvidenceArtifact`, `GateAssessment`, `DecisionRecord`, and `MutationPlan` are
linked children. The existing momentum and multi-paper results remain evidence
artifacts rather than dashboard-level subjects.

Every entity carries `stable_id`, `created_at`, `schema_version`, `input_hash`,
and `code_commit_sha`. A run also stores the three non-Chain-of-Thought
fingerprints: logic, decision trace, and environment.

`GateStatus`, `EvidenceState`, and `LifecycleDecision` are independent fields:

```text
GateStatus: PASS | FAIL | PARTIAL | NOT_RUN | UNVERIFIED
EvidenceState: CONFIRMED | NOT_CONFIRMED | CONTRADICTED
LifecycleDecision: GO | HOLD | PIVOT
```

`NOT_CONFIRMED` means the current protocol did not support promotion. It does
not prove that the hypothesis is false. Only the deterministic evaluator may
grant a PASS or GO; an LLM may propose candidates or failure explanations.

Planned versions carry `NOT_CONFIRMED` until an evidence artifact exists;
missing execution or data evidence is represented by gate states, not by
inventing a fourth evidence state. GO-only portfolio surfaces are empty until
a `PortfolioCandidate` is backed by a GO `DecisionRecord`. No live order is
generated by this dashboard.

## Consequences

- Evidence & Gates becomes a strategy detail view, preserving the existing
  metrics and provenance links.
- Failed and pivoted versions remain in the registry and evolution DAG.
- Missing PIT, exposure, capacity, and execution evidence is rendered as
  `NOT_RUN` or `UNVERIFIED`, never inferred.
- `docs/research/strategy_registry.json` is generated from committed research
  artifacts; Markdown reports are explanatory only.
